# QLS-MiCM Learning Pathways

These learning pathways group the QLS-MiCM workshops into structured tracks you can follow from foundations through to more advanced topics. Each track lists workshops in a suggested order, along with any pre-requisites.

---

## Current Workshop Inventory

The QLS-MiCM organization currently hosts **37 repositories** covering various computational biology and data science topics.
